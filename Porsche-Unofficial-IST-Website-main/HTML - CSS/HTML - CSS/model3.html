| Porsche Taycan |

The Porsche Taycan Car Model is a groundbreaking innovation
as it is Porsche's introduction of their first ever
all-electric sports car, marking a new era for Porsche. The
Taycan still delivers the speed and remarkable performance
that Porsche always aspires and accomplishes to give, but
also has a brand new elegant design, incredible handling
and acceleration. The model also has fast charging, helping
to redefine electric vehicles. It can hence be said that
this car is for anybody who wants to be sustainable, reduce
carbon emissions and have a high performing car of the future.
